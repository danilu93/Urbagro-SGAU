# 🌱 SGAU - Urban Agricultural Management System (Urbagro)

---

## 🌿 Overview
SGAU is an urban agricultural management system designed to support the organization, control, and optimization of plant cultivation processes in both small-scale and business-oriented environments.

It focuses on managing foliage, ornamental, and fruit plants, providing tools to track and record key agricultural activities in a centralized and efficient way.

---

## 🚜 Purpose
This project was created to digitalize and improve the management of agricultural processes in **Urbagro**, a real family business dedicated to urban agriculture.

SGAU replaces manual or outdated methods with a structured system that enhances traceability, reduces errors, and supports better decision-making.

---

## ⚙️ Core Features
- 🌿 Plant management (CRUD by type and category)  
- 💧 Irrigation control (dates, methods, records)  
- 🌱 Fertilization tracking (solid and liquid applications)  
- 🌾 Sowing management (process history and methods)  
- 👤 User authentication and role-based access  

---

## 📊 Additional Capabilities
- Data filtering and search by plant type or date  
- Historical records of agricultural activities  
- Input validation to reduce data errors  
- User-friendly interface  

---

## 🧱 System Design
SGAU is built with a modular and scalable architecture, allowing it to grow over time as new business needs emerge.

---

## 🚀 Future Scope
The system is expected to expand beyond operational control, including:

- Advanced production tracking  
- Analytics and reporting  
- Commercial management features (sales, inventory, and business operations)  

---

## 🧑‍💻 Technologies
- C#  
- Windows Forms  
- SQL Server  

---

## 🌍 Goal
To provide a digital solution for urban agriculture that improves efficiency, organization, and scalability for both personal and business use.
